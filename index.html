Deng Xiaoping

Deng Xiaoping was a Chinese communist leader and the most powerful figure in the People's Republic of China from the late 1970s until his death in 1997. He is my mentor because of his determination and hardwork

Jorn Lyseggen

Lyseggen started out as a research scientist in artificial intelligence and machine vision at the Norwegian Computing Center. In 2008, Lyseggen created Meltwater Foundation and launched Meltwater Entrepreneurial School of Technology (MEST) in Accra, Ghana which in 2015 was named by Fast Company as one of the top 10 most innovative organizations in Africa. He is my mentor because of his entrepreneurial mindset and the believe he has in young talents.
